📘 How to Play

Step 1: Choose a difficulty level — Easy, Medium, or Hard.

Step 2: You will get 30 seconds to play the game.

Step 3: Moles will pop up randomly on the screen.

Step 4: Click on the moles as fast as you can to hit them.

Step 5: If you hit a mole, you get points.

Step 6: Try to get the highest score before time runs out!

Have fun and give it your best shot! 🎯

💡 Did You Know?

Fact 1: Moles are insectivores, not rodents.

Fact 2: Whack-a-mole was invented in Japan in 1975.

Fact 3: A mole can dig up to 18 feet of tunnels in one hour.

Fact 4: The phrase "whack-a-mole" is used for endless problems.

Fact 5: Moles have very poor eyesight but an amazing sense of touch.

Fact 6: The largest whack-a-mole arcade machine ever built is over 10 feet tall!

Fact 7: Some mole species can tolerate low oxygen environments underground.

Fact 8: The arcade version of Whack-a-Mole was so popular it inspired video games in the 1980s.
