About Us

Our Project

Whack-a-Mole is an interactive and fun browser-based game designed to test your reflexes and speed. Our goal is to provide an engaging experience with simple yet addictive gameplay, accessible across all devices.

This project was created as part of a community-driven effort to build games that promote learning through play, featuring responsive design and user-friendly controls.

Our Contributors

This project is the result of the collective efforts of many contributors. Every single contribution, no matter how big or small, is valuable and deeply appreciated.
